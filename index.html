Experienced, Collaborative Designer

I have been working in the EdTech space for over twenty years, and as a User Experience Designer for over a decade. I believe a designer's job is not to magically produce solutions, but to lead and facilitate an inclusive process by which the team solves problems together, based on a shared understanding and commitment. I believe that the best way to support users in their varied and dynamic environments is to understand that everyone is a designer in their own way. Our job is to understand the problems our users are trying to solve, and to provide them with the right tools at the right time to enable them to design solutions.

I am based in Portland, Oregon, and am comfortable working in person and remotely. View my resume and portfolio, or contact me at singermail@gmail.com or on LinkedIn.
