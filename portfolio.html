Star CBM Data Insights

In designing the Star CBM application, we were hyper-focused on the teacher and student classroom experience, and were ruthless about excluding any features that would not directly contribute to that effort. As a result, the core product does not include analytics for school and district leaders. To avoid experience rot and to protect what's special about the core teacher experience, we decided to create a separate, admin-focused workflow centered on data and actionability.
